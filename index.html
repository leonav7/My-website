What We Offer

Our Services

From daily contract cleaning to specialist one-off jobs — we cover every corner with precision and care.

Commercial Cleaning

Contract Cleaning

Reliable, scheduled cleaning maintained under a long-term contract. Ideal for offices, schools, and retail facilities that need consistent standards.

Office Cleaning

Comprehensive office cleaning covering desks, kitchens, washrooms, and communal areas. Carried out before, during, or after business hours.

Commercial Cleaning

Large-scale facility cleaning across sectors including banking, healthcare, education, hospitality, leisure, and retail throughout the region.

Warehouse Cleaning

Industrial-standard cleaning for warehouses and logistics facilities. We handle dust control, floor scrubbing, and waste management at scale.

High Level Cleaning

Safe and thorough cleaning of high-reach areas — ceilings, beams, vents, lights, and signage — using specialist equipment and trained operatives.

Hard Floor Cleaning

Deep scrubbing, stripping, sealing, and polishing of hard floors including tile, marble, vinyl, and wood — restoring shine and extending lifespan.

Specialist & One-Off Services

Deep Cleaning

A thorough top-to-bottom clean reaching areas missed in routine cleans — behind appliances, inside fixtures, grout, vents, and hard-to-access surfaces.

After Builders Cleaning

Post-construction and renovation cleaning removing dust, debris, paint splashes, adhesive residue, and construction waste to handover-ready standard.

Carpet Cleaning

Hot water extraction, dry cleaning, and stain treatment for all carpet types. We remove embedded dirt, allergens, and odours, extending carpet life.

Window Cleaning

Internal and external window cleaning using pure water-fed pole systems and traditional methods. Streak-free results for offices, facades, and shopfronts.

End of Tenancy Cleaning

Full property cleans to meet landlord and letting agency standards — ensuring deposit recovery and a pristine handover for incoming tenants.

One-Off Cleaning

No contract required. Book a single professional clean for any space — whether for a special event, a seasonal refresh, or an urgent situation.
